About

I am a student of Systems Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am interested in the confluence of Systems and AI, particularly in leveraging parallel computing across distributed systems to train large language models (LLMs). To pursue this interest, I am currently studying CUDA to write efficient systems-level code and utilize the parallel computing power of GPUs.
